Copilot

Microsoft Copilot is a conversational, AI-powered assistant designed to increase productivity by automating routine tasks, creating content, and providing data insights. As of 2026, it is integrated across Microsoft's ecosystem, including Windows, the Microsoft 365 suite, and specialized business platforms

Perplexity

Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search engine and research assistant that provides direct, conversational answers backed by real-time web citations. Unlike traditional search engines that provide a list of links, Perplexity synthesizes information from multiple sources into a single response

Chatgpt

ChatGPT is a multimodal AI assistant developed by OpenAI that, as of early 2026, has evolved from a simple chatbot into a "personal super-assistant" capable of managing complex daily tasks. It is currently powered by the GPT-5 model family, which features advanced reasoning and agentic capabilities.

Gemini

Google Gemini is a system of AI models and assistants. As of early 2026, it replaced Google Assistant as the main AI on Android and other devices. It uses the Gemini 3 model family, which focuses on advanced reasoning, agent capabilities, and integration across Google's services.
